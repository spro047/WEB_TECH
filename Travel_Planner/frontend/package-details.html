Goa Beach Paradise

4 Days / 3 Nights Goa, India 4.5 (234 Reviews)

Overview

Experience the perfect blend of sun, sand, and sea with our Goa Beach Paradise package. This 4-day, 3-night getaway takes you to the most beautiful beaches in Goa, where you can relax, unwind, and create unforgettable memories.

From pristine beaches to vibrant nightlife, from water sports to delicious seafood, Goa has it all. Our carefully curated package ensures you get the best of what this coastal paradise has to offer.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Goa - Beach Hopping

Morning: Arrive at Goa airport/railway station. Transfer to hotel. Check-in and freshen up.

Afternoon: Visit Calangute Beach - known as the "Queen of Beaches". Enjoy water sports activities.

Evening: Explore Baga Beach and enjoy the vibrant nightlife at Tito's Lane.

Meals: Breakfast at hotel

Day 2

North Goa Exploration

Morning: Visit Fort Aguada and enjoy panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.

Afternoon: Explore Anjuna Beach and visit the famous Anjuna Flea Market.

Evening: Sunset at Vagator Beach followed by dinner at a beachside restaurant.

Meals: Breakfast at hotel, Dinner

Day 3

South Goa & Spice Plantation

Morning: Visit a spice plantation and enjoy a traditional Goan lunch.

Afternoon: Explore Colva Beach and visit the famous Our Lady of Mercy Church.

Evening: Leisure time at the beach or optional cruise on Mandovi River.

Meals: Breakfast at hotel, Lunch

Day 4

Departure

Morning: Check-out from hotel. Last-minute shopping at local markets.

Afternoon: Transfer to airport/railway station for departure.

Meals: Breakfast at hotel
