Manali Adventure Trip

5 Days / 4 Nights Manali, HP 4.7 (189 Reviews)

Overview

Embark on a thrilling adventure to the snow-capped mountains of Manali. This 5-day trip offers the perfect mix of adventure, nature, and relaxation. From skiing in Solang Valley to exploring the charming Mall Road, experience the best of the Himalayas.

Ideal for adventure seekers and nature lovers, this package encompasses scenic splendor and adrenaline-pumping activities in the lap of nature.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Manali

Morning: Arrive at Manali bus station. Transfer to hotel. Check-in and rest.

Evening: Walk around the Mall Road and visit Hadimba Devi Temple.

Meals: Dinner at hotel

Day 2

Solang Valley Adventure

Morning: Head to Solang Valley for adventure sports like Paragliding, Zorbing, and Skiing (seasonal).

Afternoon: Picnic lunch at the valley.

Evening: Return to hotel and bonfire.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Day 3

Rohtang Pass / Snow Point

Full Day: Excursion to Rohtang Pass (subject to permit and weather). Enjoy snow activities and breathtaking views.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Day 4

Kullu & Manikaran

Morning: Drive to Kullu for River Rafting.

Afternoon: Visit Manikaran Gurudwara and Hot Springs.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Day 5

Departure

Morning: Check-out. Visit Vashisht Hot Water Springs if time permits.

Afternoon: Transfer to bus station for onward journey.

Meals: Breakfast
