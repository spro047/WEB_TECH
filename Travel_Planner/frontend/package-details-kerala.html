Kerala Backwaters

6 Days / 5 Nights Kerala, India 4.8 (312 Reviews)

Overview

Discover the serene backwaters, misty hill stations, and rich culture of Kerala, "God's Own Country". This 6-day tour takes you from the rolling tea gardens of Munnar to the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Kochi - Transfer to Munnar

Morning: Arrive at Kochi Airport. Drive to Munnar (4 hrs), enjoying scenic waterfalls en route.

Evening: Check-in at resort and relax.

Day 2

Munnar Sightseeing

Full Day: Visit Eravikulam National Park, Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, and Eco Point.

Day 3

Munnar to Thekkady

Morning: Drive to Thekkady. Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.

Evening: Optional Elephant Ride or Spice Plantation tour.

Day 4

Thekkady to Alleppey

Afternoon: Board a traditional Houseboat in Alleppey backwaters. Lunch and Dinner on board.

Day 5

Alleppey to Kochi

Morning: Drive to Kochi. Visit Chinese Fishing Nets, St. Francis Church.

Evening: Shopping at Jew Town.

Day 6

Departure

Morning: Transfer to Kochi Airport for departure.
